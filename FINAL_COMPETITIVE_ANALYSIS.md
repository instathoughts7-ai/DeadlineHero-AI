## 🎯 Deep-Dive Comparison

### 1. DeadlineHero AI vs. Motion AI & Reclaim AI
*   **The Competitors**: Motion and Reclaim are excellent automated scheduling systems. They calculate calendar availability and slot in time-blocks based on user priorities.
*   **The Difference**: They treat the user like a robot. They assume that if they block out 4 hours on a calendar, the user will work without distraction. They ignore human factors like fatigue, procrastination, and anxiety.
*   **The DeadlineHero AI Advantage**: It introduces the **Burnout Monitor** and **Productivity Coach** agents. By tracking cognitive density and calculating a dynamic Burnout Index, the app proactively alerts the user when they are approaching burnout and offers concrete, manageable mini-steps. If the user experiences severe fatigue, **Emergency Rescue Mode** lets them reorganize their schedule with a single click.

### 2. DeadlineHero AI vs. Sunsama & Sunsama AI
*   **The Competitor**: Sunsama is a beautiful, highly manual daily planner designed to promote work-life balance and intentional planning.
*   **The Difference**: Sunsama relies entirely on the user's manual discipline to timebox, organize, and write reviews.
*   **The DeadlineHero AI Advantage**: It automates the tedious parts of Sunsama's workflow while preserving its mindful philosophy. The *Executive Planning Agent* breaks down vague tasks into structured milestones, and the *Reflection Agent* tracks real-time progress, letting the user plan with minimal friction.

### 3. DeadlineHero AI vs. Notion AI & Sunsama AI Wrappers
*   **The Competitors**: Many standard tools have recently added simple, generic "AI chat text areas" that summarize notes or write drafts.
*   **The Difference**: These text boxes cannot interact with your actual calendar, analyze cognitive density, or dynamically reschedule tasks.
*   **The DeadlineHero AI Advantage**: It features a **multi-agent pipeline** built on the modern `@google/genai` SDK. Agents pass validated, typed payloads to one another to create structured timelines, predict deadline risks, and coordinate calendar syncs, transforming AI from a basic writing helper into an active personal assistant.
